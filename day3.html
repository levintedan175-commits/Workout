Legs and Core Day

You can have the widest back and thickest arms in the world, but if your lower body looks like it belongs to a different person, the whole physique collapses. Toji doesn’t have that problem. His legs, core, and conditioning are built on the exact same “Heavenly Restriction” principle: zero wasted potential, pure functional brutality.

Legs (Quads, Hamstrings, Glutes, Calves) Tree-trunk quads, sweeping hamstrings, and diamond calves aren’t just for aesthetics — they’re the engine that launches Toji at inhuman speeds and lets him leap entire buildings in a single bound. Massive legs create a rock-solid base that makes the upper body look even bigger by comparison. They give explosive power for sprinting, jumping, kicking, and driving through tackles. When your legs are this developed, you walk differently — heavy, grounded, and ready to explode at any second.

Core (Abs, Obliques, Serratus, Lower Back, Spinal Erectors) Toji’s midsection isn’t just lean — it’s armor-plated. A thick, blocky core with visible obliques and serratus ties the upper and lower body together into one unbreakable unit. It absorbs punishment, transfers force from legs to fists, and keeps the spine safe when he’s throwing grown men through walls. This is the difference between getting folded in half and staying upright when someone actually lands a clean hit.

Conditioning (Cardiovascular System + Muscular Endurance) Heavenly Restriction gave Toji zero cursed energy… but infinite stamina. He can fight at 100 % intensity for minutes without gassing, sprint full speed while carrying weapons, and still have enough left in the tank to smile while doing it. Elite conditioning turns raw strength into usable power — no matter how strong you are, if you’re breathing out of your ass after 30 seconds, you’re done. Toji never is.
