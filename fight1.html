Muay Thai

Toji Fushiguro integrates Muay Thai into his fighting style for its raw, devastating power and versatility in close-quarters combat. Known as the "Art of Eight Limbs," Muay Thai emphasizes strikes with fists, elbows, knees, and shins, which align perfectly with Toji's superhuman strength and explosive bursts of speed. This inspirational embodiment captures Toji's primal brutality, mirroring Muay Thai's bone-crushing knee strikes and elbow storms that overwhelm opponents in unforgiving close range.
