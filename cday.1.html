Pull Day

Toji Fushiguro has a very specific kind of physique: not just “big,” but dense, predatory, and brutally functional. Wide back, thick arms, strong grip, and that hunched-forward, hunter silhouette.

Pull exercises

Chin-Ups (3×8–10)
Close Grip Arche Back Pull-Ups (4×6–8)
Supernaded Inverted Rows (4×8–10)
Single Arm Rows (3×8–10 per arm)
Bicep Curls (3×8–12)
False Grip Hang (2×failure)
