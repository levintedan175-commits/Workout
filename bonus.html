Dress like Toji

Black Tight-Fitting Short-Sleeve Shirt A classic compression or athletic-fit tee made of stretchy, moisture-wicking fabric (usually polyester/spandex blend). It hugs the torso and arms like a second skin, showing maximum muscle definition while staying tucked in during movement. Popular in combat sports, calisthenics, and streetwear for its clean, aggressive “no excess fabric” look.

White Oversized Harem / Fisherman / Aladdin Pants Extremely baggy, low-crotch pants with elastic or drawstring waist and cinched ankles. Usually made of lightweight cotton, linen, or rayon. Originally traditional Thai, Middle-Eastern, or martial-arts wear, they give total freedom for high kicks, deep squats, sprinting, and grappling. The exaggerated drop-crotch and volume make legs look even bigger when the fabric is still and create that signature “ninja/assassin” silhouette.

Long Black Fabric Belt / Sash A wide, soft cloth belt (similar to a martial-arts obi or kung-fu sash) tied around the waist and left to hang down the front. Serves both practical (keeps the loose pants secure) and stylistic purposes — the long hanging end adds dramatic movement when running or fighting and screams “traditional warrior” vibe.

Black Low-Top Sneakers (Nike Blazer-style in the render) Simple, flat-soled, minimalist sneakers. Chosen for grip, ankle mobility, and the ability to feel the ground — perfect for explosive sprints, jumps, and quick direction changes without the bulk of high-top boots. The all-black color keeps the outfit stealthy and menacing.
